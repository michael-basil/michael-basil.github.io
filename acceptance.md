## What is Agency?

I have agency when I am able to move towards fulfilling my potential.  Simply put, I perceive an empowered life situation.  This does not mean that I am completely unconstrained, rather the constraints present are liberating, can be negoatied, can be oriented into a productive situation or are acceptable given the larger view of things.  

What does it look like when I feel agency?

* My self worth is defined on my own terms
* I am able to approach paradoxical situations maintaining a state of relaxed alertness
* I am able to leverage networks, tools, resources and form strategies to move forward
* Control is known to be an illusion
* I am free from jealousy or envy
* Challenges are regularly wisdom generating and fun
* My primary thinking modality when pressured is Systems Oriented (versus Morally Judgemental)

## How do we know when Agency for ourselves or others is Hindered?

A good framing for this comes from reflecting on [The Stages of Grief - Accepting the Unacceptable](https://www.washington.edu/counseling/2020/06/08/the-stages-of-grief-accepting-the-unacceptable/).

* Denial
    * can look like: 
        * avoidance
        * procrastination
        * forgetting
        * easily distracted
        * mindless behaviors
        * keeping busy all the time
        * thinking/saying, “I’m fine” or “it’s fine” 
    * can feel like:
        * shock
        * numbness
        * confusion
        * shutting down
* Anger
    * can look like: 
        * pessimism
        * cynicism
        * sarcasm 
        * irritability
        * being aggressive or passive-aggressive
        * getting into arguments or physical fights
        * increased alcohol or drug use
    * can feel like:
        * frustration
        * impatience
        * resentment
        * embarrassment
        * rage
        * feeling out of control
* Bargaining
    * can look like:
        * ruminating on the future or past
        * over-thinking and worrying
        * comparing self to others
        * predicting the future and assuming the worst
        * perfectionism
        * thinking/saying, “I should have…” or ”If only…”
        * judgment toward self and/or others
    * can feel like:
        * guilt
        * shame
        * blame
        * fear
        * anxiety
        * insecurity
* Depression
    * can look like:
        * sleep and appetite changes
        * reduced energy
        * reduced social interest
        * reduced motivation
        * crying
        * increased alcohol or drug use
    * can feel like: 
        * sadness
        * despair
        * helplessness
        * hopelessness
        * disappointment
        * overwhelmed

Whereas:

* Acceptance
    * can look like:
        * mindful behaviors
        * engaging with reality as it is
        * “this is how it is right now”
        * being present in the moment
        * able to be vulnerable & tolerate emotions
        * assertive, non-defensive, honest communication
        * adapting, coping, responding skillfully
    * can feel like:
        * “good enough”
        * courageous
        * validation
        * self-compassion
        * pride
        * wisdom

Underneath these behaviors, patterns and manifestations are Mindsets which get reinforced through Habits.

## What is the difference between a Habit and a Practice?

Habits and Practices can both be good and or bad repeated actions or behaviors, but what makes something a practice is when we are Mindful and Intentional with our actions.

## What are some Mindset Recalibrations evolution examples?

### Previous

* Everyone is supposed to be nice
* If someone is bad they will be punished, find an authority
* When something happens I don't like, someone will fix it
* I should be able to do what I want
* I should be passive all the time

### Evolved

* People are true to their own nature
* There is typically no objective absolute judge, mediate
* Many times in life we will be on our own
* We don't control the actions of others, but have a choice in how we respond to situations
* Social dominance and hierarchy navigation is a learned skill, wisdom employs all tools depending on the trends

## What happens when we do not or are unable to Recalibrate?

* We store, instead of [Release Resentment](./resentment)
* We cannot [Channel Swaggger](./swaggger)
* We are ruled by, instead of [Embracing Fear](./fear)
* Most importantly, our [Health](./health) is negatively affected!
